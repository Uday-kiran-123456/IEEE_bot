VARDHAMAN COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

Vardhaman College of Engineering (VCE), an esteemed autonomous institution under JNTU Hyderabad, is located in the peaceful surroundings of Shamshabad, Hyderabad. Nestled within Kacharam Village in the Ranga Reddy district, the campus offers a serene and conducive environment for academic excellence, far from the hustle and bustle of the city.
The college provides a diverse portfolio of academic programs, encompassing undergraduate B.Tech courses in various streams like Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), CSE with specializations in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (CSM) and Data Science (DS), AI&ML, AI&DS, Information Technology (IT), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE), Mechanical Engineering (ME), and Civil Engineering (CE). It also offers M.Tech programs with specializations in Digital Electronics & Communication Systems (DECS), Power Electronics & Electrical Drives (PEED), Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), Structural Engineering (SE), and Engineering Design (ED). Additionally, the institution offers an MBA program and doctoral programs in CSE, ECE, EEE, CE, and ME.
VCE stands out for its academic achievements, holding an A++ grade accreditation from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), with an impressive CGPA of 3.58 out of 4. Six undergraduate engineering programs—B.Tech in ECE, EEE, CSE, ME, CE, and IT—have received Tier-1 accreditation from the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi. Furthermore, the MBA program earned NBA accreditation in 2023, reflecting the college's dedication to excellence in education and research.
The institution has made notable strides in areas such as teaching-learning practices, research, consultancy, innovation, technology transfer, community outreach, and value-based education. The campus is well-equipped with state-of-the-art academic blocks, computer centers, lecture halls, libraries, laboratories, auditoriums, guest accommodations, hostels, and student facilities. Additional amenities include a stationery store, cafeteria, CBI ATM, and a health center for the well-being of students and staff.
VCE takes pride in its accomplished faculty, who bring extensive experience and expertise across diverse fields. The college ensures its curriculum remains current and industry-relevant by regularly updating the syllabi for undergraduate and postgraduate programs. The choice-based credit system (CBCS) enables students to select from a wide array of electives, including interdisciplinary and multidisciplinary courses. Additionally, students are encouraged to engage in projects that foster creativity and critical thinking, preparing them for future challenges.
UNDER GRADUATION PROGRAM
Vardhaman College of Engineering offers a robust range of undergraduate programs with a total intake capacity of 1,380 students. The flagship B.Tech program in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) accommodates 540 students, while the specialized CSE programs in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI&ML) and Data Science (DS) each have an intake of 180 students. The Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) and Information Technology (IT) programs also admit 180 students each. Other engineering disciplines include Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) with an intake of 60 students, and both Mechanical Engineering (ME) and Civil Engineering (CE) programs with a capacity of 30 students each, catering to niche academic and industrial demands.

ADMISSION PROCESS
Admissions to Vardhaman College of Engineering are conducted through Common Entrance Tests organized by the Telangana State Council of Higher Education, Hyderabad. For undergraduate B.Tech programs, 70% of the seats (Category-A) are allotted based on ranks obtained in EAMCET, while 30% (Category-B) are reserved for other candidates. Additionally, 10% of the total sanctioned intake is allocated for lateral entry into the second year for diploma holders through ECET.
For postgraduate programs, M.Tech admissions follow a similar structure, with 70% of seats (Category-A) allocated based on PGECET ranks, giving preference to GATE-qualified candidates, and the remaining 30% (Category-B) reserved for other candidates. The MBA program also allocates 70% of its seats based on ICET ranks (Category-A), while 30% are earmarked for other candidates under Category-B.
Doctoral programs (Ph.D.) are offered in the departments of Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. Admissions to these research-oriented programs are managed through JNTUH, Hyderabad.
FEE STRUCTURE
The tuition fee structure at Vardhaman College of Engineering for four years integrated Btech program varies based on the year of admission. Students admitted in 2022 have an annual tuition fee of ₹1,40,000, while those admitted in 2021, 2020, and 2019 pay ₹1,25,000 per year. This reflects a revision in the fee structure for the most recent batch to accommodate institutional enhancements and academic advancements.
The tuition fee for the M.Tech program at Vardhaman College of Engineering remains consistent for recent batches. Students admitted in both 2022 and 2021 pay an annual tuition fee of ₹57,600, ensuring affordability while maintaining high academic standards.
The tuition fee for the MBA program at Vardhaman College of Engineering has remained unchanged for recent batches. Students admitted in both 2021 and 2020 pay an annual tuition fee of ₹45,000, reflecting the institution's commitment to providing quality management education at an affordable cost.
